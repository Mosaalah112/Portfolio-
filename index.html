About Me

I’m a fourth-year Pharmacy student at Alexandria University with a strong passion for visual creativity. I began learning graphic design in my first year of high school, and the Covid-19 quarantine gave me the time and space to grow this hobby quickly. As someone who naturally loves drawing, moving into digital tools like Photoshop and Illustrator felt like the perfect next step. Today, I consider myself an intermediate designer with a special interest in social media design and illustration.
